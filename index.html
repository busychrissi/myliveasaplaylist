My Life as a Playlist

1990 Sinéad O'Connor - Nothing Compares 2 U
This song was at No. 1 in Germany on the 23th March 1990, the day I was born. Actually I haven't known that before I started this project.
1996 Backstreet Boys - Get Down
I got their album for Christmas as a cassette together with my purple cassette recorder and running around with it made me feel like a boss.
1998 Chopin - Fantasie Imprumtu
‚
My mom played it on the piano and I decided that I need to play it one day, too. Still on my bucket list.
2000 Britney Spears - Oops! I Did It Again
My crush and superstar. And my first CD. She's coming to Germany this year and I'm going to see her the first time in my life.
2001 No Angels - Goodbye To Yesterday
I loved this girl group. They won a German Casting show and I wanted to be a superstar as well. I recorded their songs on own tapes and when I heard it I realized that I probably shouldn't sing in public.
2002 t.A.T.u - All The Things She Said
One of the pivotal moments I realized that I might be into women.
2004 System of A Down - B.Y.O.B.
My best friends were all getting punks but I was faithful to my white Sneakers. System of A Down might be the only rock band I liked and still like.
2005 Missy Elliot - Loose Control
I'm now officially in love with Hip Hop music. In my next life I wanna be Missy Elliot. Or perform together with her on stage.
2008 Deichkind - Hört ihr die Signale
BBQs in summer, celebrating carnival with my Girl Gang and driving my dads green family van.
2009 Kings of Leon - Sex on Fire
Reminds me so much of being in Brighton, England with my best friend. They played it at the end of every party, 2 a.m. Still wondering why they close their clubs when the party is at its peak.
2010 Pitbull - Maldito Alcohol
I was in Boston for 2 months learning English and having a lot of parties with people from all over the world. One crazy guy even got a tattoo of a line of this song.
2011 Klaas & Bodybangers - I Like
Spending crazy party nights dancing on beach together with an amazing friend.
2015 Global Deejays - The Sound of San Francisco
Reminds me of my great US West Coast road trip driving the car along Highway 1.
2017 Chefboss - Blitzlichtgewitter
2017 was a rough year and the songs of Chefboss saved my mood in some difficult situations. They also remind me of an amazing friend who was there for me in this time.
